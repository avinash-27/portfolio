Just a few words about me

With the aspirations of building newer technology to make the world a fun place to live in, I opted to derive a solid foundation in Computer Science. I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science whereby I learned about operating systems, data structures, software engineering, artificial intelligence and many other intricate details typically abstracted out of the naked eye, that make computers ever so increasingly efficient. To pursue my knowledge in software development, I undertook certification programs in Java, C, C++, IOS App Dev and Web development. Apart from this I am also proficient with adobe creative cloud applications such as photoshop, illustrator, lightroom, premier pro and finalcut pro. I consider myself as a good capable Software Engineer with an array of credentials to support this statement. Complementing my diverse skill set, I have developed an excellent understanding of a wide range of programming languages, tools and have demonstrated this capability throughout my undergraduate and my ongoing Computer Software Engineering Master’s degree from George Mason University.
